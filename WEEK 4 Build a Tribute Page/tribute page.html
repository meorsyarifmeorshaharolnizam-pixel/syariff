About Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was born in 1856 and became one of history's greatest inventors. He developed the alternating current (AC) electrical system that powers homes and industries around the world today.

Throughout his career, Tesla designed many groundbreaking inventions in electricity, wireless communication, and engineering. His creativity and scientific vision continue to inspire engineers and inventors.

His contributions remain essential to modern technology and electrical power systems.
